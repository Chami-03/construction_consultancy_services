About Prismo Constructions

Building excellence through expertise, integrity, and innovation

A Consultancy Built on Best Practice

Prismo Constructions was established to bridge strategic planning and practical project execution. Our consultants bring multidisciplinary expertise across engineering, commercial management, and quality systems.

We support clients in making informed project decisions at every milestone, ensuring alignment with performance targets, compliance obligations, and international standards.

From feasibility to handover, our focus remains consistent: certainty, resilience, and measurable project excellence.

  • International standards and best-practice delivery
  • Strong cost, schedule, and risk governance
  • Transparent stakeholder communication
  • Experienced multidisciplinary advisory team
  • Proven outcomes across sectors in Sri Lanka
